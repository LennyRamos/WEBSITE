Why I Love What I Do

My interest in cyberspace/computing first began with video games, most notably Megaman. Megaman Battle Network 3 for the Gameboy Advanced was more focused on networks rather than robots. You had a PET (Personal Terminal) which contained a customizable artificial intelligence, know as a NetNavi. MegaMan.exe, who was your NetNavi, helped you surf the internet and battle against viruses. The NetNavi protected itself using defensive programming already built into them but as the Navi operator you also had the ability to send weapon programs from the PET via BattleChips.
